ABOUT ECM DEPARTMENT

		
The department of Electronics and Computer Engineering
(ECM) was started with an objective of meeting the hardware &
software expertise required for modern applications. The ECM
department started in the year 1999 with an initial intake of only
20 students. The department has been growing in strength and
stature ever since. Currently, the department is graduating 120
students every year where in 85% of the student are selected for
campus placements. The department offers core subjects related
to both hardware and software components of IT industry.This
enables students to gain knowledge and benefits in both the
areas. The department is first in JNTUH to be qualified and
accredited by AICTE for five years in the very first attempt,
mainly due to the course structure framed by experience faculty
and industry experts for its B.Tech course.
The department has faculty from R & D organization, Industry
and other reputed academic institutions which comes as an asset
for the students of the department in acquiring the theoretical
and practical knowledge of the subjects they are studying. The
students of the department are the most sought after by the IT
industry due to their skills in both hardware and software. The
students of ECM have made remarkable achievements by
winning prizes in the technical competitions, filing patents in
collaboration with reputed institutes and working on sponsored
internship programs. The excellent performance of the alumni
of the department is an equally important factor for the
successful running of the department.

Vision:

		

To emerge as a premier centre in Electronics and Computer engineering
with focus on human values and professional ethics

Mission:

		  
    

    

  • To prepare Electronics and Computer Engineering graduates to be a life- long 
learner with competence in basic sciences, engineering & professional core,
interdisciplinary subjects, so that they can have professional career or to pursue
higher studies.

    • 

  • Developing liaison with Academia, R & D institutions, software and electronics 
Industries for exposure of students to the practical aspects in engineering and
solution of the industry oriented and societal problems, entrepreneurial pursuit
and project management

    • 

  • Inculcating interpersonal skills, team work, professional ethics, IPR and
regulatory issues in students to improve their employability and promoting leadership
in changing global environment

    • 

  • To continuously engage in research and development activities and to promote
scientific temper in the graduates
