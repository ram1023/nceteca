Sponsored by Sree Education Society of the Sree Group of Industries, Sreenidhi Institute of Science & Technology,
Hyderabad is shaped into one of the best among the Technological Institutions in INDIA. Sponsored by Sree Education Society of the Sree Group of Industries, Sreenidhi Institute of Science & Technology, Hyderabad is shaped into one of the best among the Technological Institutions in INDIA.

Sreenidhi has made a mark in the short span of its existence due to enormous efforts put in by the management and the staff. Every effort is made to develop necessary infrastructure, teaching learning process, support services and facilities for extra curricular activities in an exemplary manner. Senior Professors who are well known in their own branches of engineering have laid a solid foundation for building this institution. Their enormous experience made Sreenidhi, an institution par excellence in the technical education system of India.

The management of Sreenidhi is very enlightened and dynamic. Any initiative that is required is always forth coming and necessary finances are provided for building the institution. State of the art laboratories, library with latest books and periodicals and computer centre with high ended systems in Sreenidhi provide an excellent environment for serious studies.

Sreenidhi has excellent staff and they have the means by which effective instruction can be carried out. Every class room has overhead projector facilities and teachers use transparency banks for teaching purpose. There is a novel and unique Best Teacher Award Scheme which is motivating the teachers to do their best non only in teaching but also in building up of the institution.

The Students are provided excellent opportunities for improving their communication skills. An hour is provided every week for presentation of seminars. Students are also encouraged to participate in various technical paper meets and other contests. They have brought laurels to the institution.

On account of academic results of our students in the University Examinations, quality of students joining Sreenidhi and innumerable occasions in which they have made Sreenidhi proud; is what made Indian Society for Training and Development, New Delhi to give us National Award on Best HRD Practices and why Sreenidhi is proud to be numero uno are also explained.
